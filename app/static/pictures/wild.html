    Timeline[edit]

    • 2018
    • Show All
    • February 5th - Team Wild sign their first CS:GO team consisting of: viol, rAncrow, GYZER, DANVIET, and betoow. [1]
    • February 8th - landic joins. [2]
    • April 23rd - betoow leaves Team Wild. [3]
    • April 27th - Brnz1k joins Team Wild after leaving VNS Sushi Rão. [4]
    • May 10th - gZERO joins the team as their new coach. [5]
    • August 13th - Team Wild release their CS:GO squad. [6]
    • August 17th - Team Wild sign Virtue Gaming's CS:GO team. [7]
